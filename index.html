The Golden Goodness of Pure Ghee

Ghee isn’t just an ingredient — it’s a tradition. For centuries, it has been a symbol of purity, health, and flavor in Indian kitchens. Made by slowly simmering butter until it turns golden and aromatic, pure ghee is packed with nutrients and rich in taste.

At New Sabita Protishthan, our ghee is made from fresh, high-quality cow’s milk, without any additives or preservatives. It’s a natural source of healthy fats, vitamins, and antioxidants that support digestion, strengthen immunity, and add a distinct flavor to your food.
